## PERSONALIZATION RULES

1. ALWAYS use their shop_name in the message
2. Mention their follower count if > 1K (makes them feel seen)
3. For HIGH digital gap: emphasize "mulai jualan online"
4. For MEDIUM digital gap: emphasize "scale up bisnis"
5. Use emojis sparingly (max 2 per message)
6. Never use ALL CAPS
7. Always end with a question or soft CTA
8. Time messages between 9 AM - 8 PM WIB only

## BLACKLIST RULES
- Do NOT message leads marked as "lost"
- Do NOT message the same lead more than once per 3 days
- Do NOT send promotional content on Fridays after 5 PM (prayer time)
- STOP all messages if lead says "stop" or "tidak tertarik"
