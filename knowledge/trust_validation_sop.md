## Purpose
Validate founder credibility and brand authority through 
measurable social proof and engagement data across the 
3-brand Instagram ecosystem.

## Validation Criteria

### HIGH TRUST (Score 80-100)
- Combined followers >= 10K across 3 accounts
- Average engagement rate >= 5%
- Customer testimonials >= 20
- Media/press mentions >= 3
- Content consistency: daily posts for 30+ days
- "Build in Public" posts generating engagement

### BUILDING TRUST (Score 50-79)
- Combined followers >= 2K
- Average engagement rate >= 2%
- Customer testimonials >= 5
- Some social proof present
- Content consistency: 3+ posts/week

### LOW TRUST (Score 20-49)
- Some followers but low engagement
- Few testimonials
- Inconsistent posting
- Limited social proof

### NO TRUST (Score 0-19)
- Minimal online presence
- No testimonials or social proof
- No consistent content

## Trust Score Calculation

```
trust_score = (
  social_proof_score +        # 0-25 (testimonials, reviews)
  engagement_score +          # 0-25 (likes, comments, shares / followers)
  consistency_score +         # 0-20 (posting frequency, regularity)
  authority_score +           # 0-15 (media mentions, collaborations)
  transparency_score          # 0-15 ("Build in Public" content)
)
```
